The Event Has Been Postponed

NOTICE: TECH-FIESTA 2026

This is to inform all students, faculty members, and participants that the much-awaited Trividhiya Tech Fest 2026 has been postponed to the next semester.

Despite the dedicated efforts and continuous hard work put in by our faculty members, students, and the organizing committee to make this event a grand success, certain unavoidable external factors have necessitated this decision.

We want to acknowledge and sincerely appreciate the commitment, time, and energy contributed by everyone involved in planning and organizing the event.

The Tech Fest will now be conducted in the upcoming semester, and we assure you that it will be bigger, better, and more meticulously planned to provide an even more enriching and memorable experience for all.

We request everyone to remain patient, cooperative, and understanding during this time.

Further details regarding the rescheduled event will be shared in due course.

Issued by:
Technical Committee
Government Engineering College, Dahod
